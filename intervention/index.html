Title: A Rice Bag Intervention

Description:

I listed a royal basmati rice bag on Mercari, a webiste like eBay used to sell secondhand items. Many people are selling items for outstanding prices so I decided I would also list something super weird. Below is an image of the account I created

Intervention:

Below are the images of my listing, with the price, description. I decided to price it extrordinarily high to see what would happen. I made sure to add the keyword 'vintage' to see if it would increase and clicks or views. I initially listed it at $50 but was able to turn on a price fluctuating feature that would allow for the price change based on the amount of views and interest.

Effects:

After having the lisitng up, below are the results. At first I was able to see similar items that were listed and I was surprised to see there were similar items up. By today, my item has gotten 9 views and no bids which I expected at this atrocius price for a mere rice bag. So far I haven't gotten any comments.

Future Expansion:

If I had more time I would modify the listing. Maybe if I could significantly lower the price I would be able to sell the item. With added time the post would be able to gain more traction and maybe even some more comments.
